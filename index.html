Biography

Discover something about me

Growing Up

I was born on September 22, 1999, in the small town of Milici, Bosnia and Herzegovina. As a child, I was curious and full of energy, always exploring the world around me. When I was just five years old, my family and I moved to the city of Bijeljina, where I began my life journey and made new friends.

Education

I completed my elementary education at "Jovan Ducic" primary school in Bijeljina in 2014. During my school days, I showed exceptional learning abilities and achieved excellent results. After finishing elementary school, I enrolled in gymnasium, which I successfully completed in 2018. In 2021, I enrolled in the Novi Sad School of Business. Majoring in Applied Informatics. I am currently in my second year of studies.

Career

In 2022, I started working at an IT company called Cyberlab. Initially, I was an intern for three months, and then I received a job offer. Currently, I work in the same company as a DevOps engineer.

Football career

I started playing football when I was 9 years old. I played for clubs FK Sloga United, Zvijezda 09, OFK Sloga GC, FK Drina Batar. Currently, I am playing for CSK from Celarevo. The greatest achievements I have accomplished are second place in the Premier League of Bosnia and Herzegovina for juniors, Champion of the Second League of Republika Srpska, Champion of the First Municipal League Bijeljina, Champion of the Regional League Semberija.
